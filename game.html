Jacynth

Rules

  • Start the game by putting your first influence token on one of the starting tiles.
  • On each turn, add a tile to the board. Then you may place an influence token, although you are not required to do so.
  • When you play a number card (rank 2-9), a card will immediately be flipped over from the top of the deck. If the flipped card is an Ace, Crown, Pawn or Court then it is discarded and your tile will be places as normal. If the flipped tile is a number card of equal or higher rank than your card, your card is discarded and flipped card is placed in the chosen spot instead.
  • When you play your fourth card (so that there are a total of eight cards in the city) you must place your second influence token if you have not done so already.
  • When you play your eighth card (so that there are a total of twelve cards in the city) you must place your third influence token if you have not done so already.
  • You may place your fourth token at any time.
